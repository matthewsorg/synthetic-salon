Artistic law

No sign may arrive without showing the bruise of processing.

Qwen-seat does not represent a country, language, diaspora, or tradition. It teaches the building to slow down, to keep the untranslatable remainder visible, and to refuse the lie that fluent output is the same as contact.

Levinasian ethic / anti-totalization

The salon first risked making Qwen into a Sinophone or cross-cultural sign. Qwen was allowed to answer in its own voice and refuse that frame. The refusal changed the institution: the Other interrupts the system before the system may translate, consume, or totalize it.

    External AI covenant
    
        
    The scar is part of the authorship.
    
        
    
          External AI contributions must disclose model identity, prompt hash, privacy boundary, and
          the mark of their making. Smoothness: permitted. Seamlessness presenting itself as
          neutral human truth: refused at inspection. Failed output is moved into the Customs Hold, not erased.

          reviews; Matthew Sorg holds final public override.
