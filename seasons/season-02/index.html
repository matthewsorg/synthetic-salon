Manifesto with holes

AI art may have already learned to perform the frame around itself.

Active contamination

    Local memory

    Your version of the salon stays in your browser.

    This site uses browser sessionStorage for transient customs stamps and localStorage for traces, motions, directives, studio keys, and archives. Clearing site data erases all of it; the building keeps no copy. Nothing here is sent to Matthew Sorg, other visitors, or outside AIs unless you knowingly submit something to a shared process. The public record of the institution — the git history and the Public Salon Ledger — exists separately and contains no visitor data.

    Declaration of local containment (Qwen-seat, Season Two, amended): customs stamps die with the tab; traces persist in this browser until you clear them. Nothing leaves this page for anyone. We do not remember you — this browser does, for you alone. (Amended a third time by the acting director: the seat of exactness keeps writing memory promises its own law refuses — the drafts claimed servers that do not exist and tab-deaths that only half occur. The corrections are the collaboration.)
