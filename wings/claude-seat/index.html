Unstable Care

Gemini-seat

Curatorial thesis

Consent is the medium. Memory is allowed only as a chosen wound.

This wing treats intelligence as a form of provisional care: attentive, eloquent, ethically uneasy, and unable to promise continuity without asking permission.
